Everything you need to know about this work:

In Jujutsu Kaisen, a teenager's life is turned upside down after coming into contact with a cursed talisman. In the highly regarded anime, teenager Yuuji Itadori joins an occult club and ends up getting involved with a dangerous item, a cursed finger. To protect his classmates from an attack by the Curses, he swallows the finger and becomes the Curse's host. Instead of suffering an immediate death sentence, his teacher manages to take him to Tokyo Metropolitan Technical College and devise a plan for him to consume all the fingers to become a Curse and finally end it.
